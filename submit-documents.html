Required Documents

General Document Requirements

Make sure you have these documents ready before starting your application

For Visa Applications

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport biodata page scan
  • Recent passport photograph (white background)
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel booking or invitation)
  • Travel itinerary or flight details
  • Proof of financial means
  • Host invitation letter (if applicable)
  • Host identification documents (if applicable)

For Passport Applications

  • Recent passport photograph (white background)
  • Current passport (for renewal applications)
  • Birth certificate (for first-time applications)
  • Parents' passports (for minors)
  • Affidavit for lost/stolen passports
  • Change of name deed (if applicable)
  • Police report (for lost passports)
  • Parental consent affidavit (for single parents)
Important Note

Additional documents may be required based on your specific situation. Our team will guide you through the exact requirements during the application process.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does the visa application process take?

Our standard processing time is 10 working days from the date of complete submission. This includes document verification, application submission, and interview scheduling if required.

What if I don't have all the required documents?

You can start your application with the documents you have. Our team will review your submission and provide guidance on any missing documents. We can also advise on alternative documents that may be acceptable.

Is the service fee refundable if my application is rejected?

Our service fee covers the assistance and processing work we do on your behalf. While we strive for 100% success rate, if your application is rejected due to circumstances beyond our control, the service fee is non-refundable. However, we provide detailed feedback and can assist with reapplication.

Can I apply for multiple family members together?

Yes, you can apply for multiple family members in a single application. We offer family discounts for visa applications. For passport applications, each individual needs a separate application, but you can submit them together for convenience.

How do I know if my documents are acceptable?

Our system will validate your documents during upload. Additionally, our team reviews all submissions within 24 hours and will contact you if any documents need to be resubmitted or if additional documents are required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept secure payments through PayPal. All transactions are encrypted and secure. You'll be redirected to the payment gateway after completing your application form.
